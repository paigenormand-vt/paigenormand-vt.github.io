Computing Camp for Refugees

Computing Camp for Refugees

From 2019–2023, I led the Computer Science Department's week-long summer camp for local refugee students, coordinating coding with TwoVille, Python programming, and VR design tracks.

I coordinated with CS faculty, the Harrisonburg CWS Refugee Center, and JMU's Youth Safety Office. I recruited and trained undergraduate CS students to serve as mentors. In 2024, our department was recognized with CWS Harrisonburg's Youth STEM Partner of the Year award for our continued investment and support of their students.
